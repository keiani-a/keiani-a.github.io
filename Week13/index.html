About

Independent and based in Oakland, CA since 2017, Krause Avenue is a Black-owned clothing brand created by Keiani Allen. Krause values sustainability, social justice, and breaking social norms.

The brand is known for its irreverence and disdain for respectability politics, reflected in the playful, child-like, and sometimes mildly inappropriate designs. Each piece is made to be comfortable, functional, and an extension of the wearer.
